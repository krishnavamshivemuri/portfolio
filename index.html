Powder Bed Fusion (PBF) Additive Manufacturing Simulation

COMSOL Additive Manufacturing Thermal Analysis Material Science

ANSYS FEA Thermal Analysis

Designed and studied the mechanical properties of additive manufactured ABS and PETG thermoplastic Tensile and Impact test specimens. Conducted an examination of field test data pertaining to Tensile and Impact specimens, to pinpoint areas necessitating enhancement, and optimized the FDM printin parameters.
